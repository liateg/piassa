Piassa Restorant & Mart

A Traditional Ethiopian dining experience

Located in Scottdale, Piassa offers a traditional Ethiopian experience seven days a week for lunch and dinner.

Journey to Ethiopia: Savor Authentic Flavors and Vibrant Culture in Atlanta

Savor an authentic collection of Ethiopian fare in our sprawling dining room inspired by the vast cultural and social scenes of Ethiopia. We bring Atlanta a hearty, farm fresh menu paired with lively native entertainment from talented acts that will transport you straight to Addis Ababa. Come and enjoy the most authentic Ethiopian Restaurant in US.

Indulge in Ethiopian Tradition: Sip on Tej (Honey Wine) and Tela

Event Space

Piassa is the perfect place for your next private event. Comfortably holding up to 120 people for a seated or buffet dinner, your options are nearly limitless. We will create a customized menu tailored to suit your budget and the tastes of your guests.
